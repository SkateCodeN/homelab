Purpose

This is the custom splash page for the homelab web app. So far we have a pfsense firewall installed. On our firewall we have a few ports forwarded and we have our DNS passing through cloudflare.

I think this is all happened with my desire to work on IT and software related activities. My background is both from IT Networking and software development. Now is the time to sort of put it all together, to have fun.

A memory from the past came to me when typing the above. It was in Sacramento in my days as a high school student. I was in a CCNA competion for Skills USA. The regional jugde was telling me about a student that was running his own server at home. Not only that but this person had CISCO switches and routers to practice his internetworking skills. I just thought that was the coolest thing. Now that I am older, I have the means and knowlege to do the same. To develop an ecosystem of software I create. Empashis on starting something. I have a tendancy to not finish my projects.
